Works

A selection of our projects

New builds, renovations and interiors across a range of scales, each shaped by its site and its people. Every project below reflects the same process — careful listening, considered design and hands-on delivery from first sketch to final handover.

Birch House

Private Residence · New Build Oxfordshire · 2023 · 240m²

A light-filled family home organised around a sheltered courtyard and pool. Polished concrete floors and full-height glazing carry the main living space out onto the garden, with timber cladding softening the elevation as it meets the trees behind.

Foldgate Barn

Renovation Cotswolds · 2022 · 310m²

A tired agricultural barn reworked into a modern family home. The original brick shell was retained and repointed, with a glazed timber-clad link connecting it to a new kitchen and living wing that opens onto the courtyard.

Willow Studio

New Build Surrey · 2021 · 32m²

A garden studio for work and quiet retreat, wrapped in cedar cladding that will silver with age. Deep roof overhangs shade the glazing in summer, while underfloor heating keeps it usable through the colder months.

Ashfield Extension

Extension Berkshire · 2023 · 45m²

A single-storey rear extension that opens a 1930s semi onto the garden through full-height sliding glass. A rooflight over the new dining area keeps the plan bright even on the shortest winter days.

Meadow Row

Interior Architecture London · 2022 · 180m²

A full interior reconfiguration for a small architecture practice, bringing daylight deep into the plan through a reworked stair core and a new rooflight above the central meeting space.

The Old Granary

Renovation Suffolk · 2020 · 260m²

A former grain store carefully restored and adapted into a contemporary family home. Original oak framing was exposed and repaired, with new joinery detailed to sit quietly alongside it.

Larch Cottage

Private Residence · New Build Hampshire · 2024 · 140m²

A compact new-build cottage designed around a sheltered, south-facing terrace and long views over open farmland. Larch cladding and a simple pitched form keep it modest against the landscape.

Crescent Mews

Extension London · 2021 · 90m²

A first-floor addition and full remodel giving a mews house a generous new main bedroom suite, along with a reworked stair and a small roof terrace looking back over the city.

Hollow Farm

Renovation Devon · 2023 · 220m²

A farmhouse renovation balancing period character with a considered, low-energy retrofit — internal wall insulation, an air source heat pump and secondary glazing throughout, without losing the original detailing.
