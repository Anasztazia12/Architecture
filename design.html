Process

How a Project Unfolds

Our services follow the shape of a typical project, loosely aligned with the RIBA Plan of Work, so you always know what stage you're at and what comes next. Every phase below is handled in-house, from the first feasibility sketch through to the final account on site.

01

Feasibility & Brief

Site analysis, budget testing and a clear brief that sets the direction for everything that follows.

02

Concept Design

Early massing and layouts exploring how the project could take shape.

03

Planning Permission

Preparing and submitting the application, then managing it through to consent.

04

Technical Design

Detailed drawings that resolve construction, structure and building regulations.

05

Building Control & Tender

Formal approval alongside contractor pricing, so the project is costed before it's built.

06

Construction Drawings

Fully coordinated information issued to the contractor for site.

07

Contract Administration

Overseeing the build on your behalf, from first fix through to final account.

Alongside every phase, we also offer 3D design and visualisation, helping you see a project clearly before a single brick is laid.
